Day 2 · Sat Jul 12 Grand Teton National Park
  • 7:00 AM
    Early Start — Jenny Lake
    Drive to Jenny Lake (30 min from Jackson). Take the ferry across ($20 RT) to cut 2 miles off the Hidden Falls / Inspiration Point hike. Arrive early to beat crowds and find parking.
    ⛴ Ferry runs 7 AM–7 PM · Cash or card
  • 9:00 AM
    Inspiration Point Hike
    ~4.5 miles round trip with ferry. Moderate difficulty. Stunning views of Jenny Lake and the Tetons. Allow 3–4 hours.
    🥾 Bring bear spray & plenty of water
  • 1:00 PM
    Signal Mountain Summit Road
    Drive the 5-mile summit road for a panoramic view of the entire valley and Jackson Lake. No hiking required.
  • 3:00 PM
    Oxbow Bend Wildlife Viewing
    Prime spot for moose, bald eagles, and great blue herons along the Snake River. Bring binoculars.
    🦅 Best wildlife early morning or evening
  • 7:00 PM
    Dinner + Sunset
    Return to Jackson for dinner. Catch sunset at Schwabacher Landing for iconic Teton reflections if sky is clear.
    🌅 Schwabacher Landing: 4 miles S of Moose Junction
